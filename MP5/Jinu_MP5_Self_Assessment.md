|-------|--------|------|
| `slides.pdf` exported (not editable) and pushed to `MP5/` | ☐ | export from your deck tool to PDF |
| Repo URL submitted on Canvas | ☐ | the night before your session |

| 15-min timing rehearsed out loud | ☐ | aim 13–14 min to leave Q&A room |
| Backup of slides on a USB / cloud link | ☐ | insurance |

## The "we forgot to upload the deck" guard
The two things that lose points for reasons unrelated to engineering: (1) deck not
exported to PDF / not pushed before the deadline, (2) demo or MCP call not tested on the
room's setup. Both are on this list — clear them first.
